About the App

THIS EXCITING NEW APP is simplistic yet USEFUL! If you are a DIYer or just need an app that makes up for the holes in your toolbox, THIS IS THE APP FOR YOU!

Features & Functionality

  • Level: Turns your device into a precision level, complete with angle readings in degrees.
  • Socket Size: Displays a chart of common metric and imperial socket sizes, showing which are compatible or interchangeable.
  • Compass: A simple compass with cardinal directions, city/state info, and latitude/longitude. Location data is not stored.
